How much space is required for a golf simulator?

It depends on your swing. Many golfers are comfortable using a golf simulator with dimensions of 3 meters in width 4 meters in depth, and 3 meters in height. While some manage to fit simulators into smaller spaces, most indoor golfers aim to maximize the available space.

Minimum space requirements

  • Width - 2.5 metres
  • Depth - 2.5 metres
  • Height - 2.5 metres

Ideal space requirements

  • Width - 3 metres
  • Depth - 4 metres
  • Height - 3 metres
